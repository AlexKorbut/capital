# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 9 June 2026_

> **Template.** This text is a starting point and **must be reviewed by a lawyer**
> before a public launch (including for GDPR and local-law compliance).

Privacy is at the core of KAPITAL. We collect the minimum and protect it.

## 1. What data we process
- **Account:** email, name (optional), password hash, base currency.
- **Financial data:** the assets you enter, portfolio snapshots, notes, wallet
  addresses, raw input text. Sensitive fields (wallet addresses, notes, raw input)
  are **encrypted at the application level** (AES/Fernet).
- **Payments:** customer and event identifiers from payment providers (Stripe,
  CoinGate). **We never receive or store bank-card data** — it is handled by Stripe
  (PCI DSS).
- **Technical data:** logs, IP address, request identifiers — for security and
  diagnostics.

## 2. Why
To provide and improve the Service, portfolio analytics, security, and to fulfil
payments and legal obligations.

## 3. AI processing
To produce insights and parse input, data may be sent to the configured LLM
provider. Only the necessary minimum is sent; the provider is named in the Service
configuration.

## 4. Sharing with third parties
We do not sell your data. We share only with processors required to run the Service
(hosting, payments, email, the LLM provider, monitoring), under contract.

## 5. Storage and security
Data is stored with field encryption where appropriate; access is restricted.
Transport is protected with TLS (HTTPS). We apply reasonable organisational and
technical measures.

## 6. Your rights (GDPR)
- **Export:** download all your data in a machine-readable form
  (`Settings → Export data`).
- **Deletion:** full deletion of the account and related data
  (`Settings → Delete account`) — this is irreversible.
- Access, rectification, restriction and objection to processing — on request.

## 7. Cookies
We use only technically necessary local storage for the session. No advertising
tracking.

## 8. Changes
We will notify you of changes to this Policy within the Service.

## 9. Contact
For privacy questions — via the in-app support form.
